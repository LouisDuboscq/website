## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Simple body measurement app (“the App”) handles user data.

The App is designed as a **simple body measurement logging tool** and follows a **privacy-by-design** approach.

---

## No Data Collection

**The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

Specifically:

- No personal information
- No health data collected by us
- No analytics
- No tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No third-party SDKs collecting data
- No data sent to external servers

**All data remains on the user’s device.**

---

## No Account Creation

The App does **not** require account creation.

- No registration
- No login
- No user identification
- No profiles

We do not know who uses the App and cannot identify individual users.

---

## Local-Only Storage

Any information you choose to enter (such as body measurements or notes) is:

- Stored **only on your device**
- Never uploaded or synchronized
- Never accessible to us

Deleting the App will permanently delete all locally stored data.

---

## Data Access & Deletion Requests

Since the App does not collect or store data externally:

- We cannot access your data
- We cannot retrieve, export, or delete data on request
- We cannot respond to GDPR / CCPA data access or deletion requests, as no data is held by us

All data control remains with the user via their own device.

---

## Non-Medical Use Disclaimer

**Simple body measurement app is not a medical application or medical device.**

- The App is provided for **informational and personal tracking purposes only**
- It does **not** provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
- It does **not** assess health conditions or risks
- It does **not** replace professional medical advice

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical or health-related concerns.

---

## Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children.

As no data collection occurs, no personal or sensitive data related to children is processed.

- The App provides no guarantees regarding accuracy, outcomes, or interpretations

Use of the App is entirely at your own risk.
