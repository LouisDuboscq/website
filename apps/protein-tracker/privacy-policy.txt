## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Protein Tracker app** (“the App”) handles user data.

Your privacy is extremely important to us, and the App is designed with a **privacy-first approach**.

---

## Data Collection

**Protein Tracker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

- No personal information is collected
- No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used
- No data is sent to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties

**Zero data collected.**

---

## Account Creation

The App **does not require account creation**.

As a result:

- We do not know who you are
- We cannot identify individual users
- We cannot access, retrieve, export, or delete your data on request

All data entered in the App remains **only on your device**.

---

## Local Data Storage

Any data you enter (such as protein intake, food logs, or personal notes) is:

- Stored **locally on your phone**
- Never uploaded or synchronized
- Fully under your control

Deleting the App will permanently delete all associated data from your device.

---

## Data Deletion & Access Requests

Since **no data is collected or stored externally**, we:

- Cannot access your data
- Cannot delete or export your data on your behalf
- Cannot respond to data access or deletion requests (GDPR/CCPA), as we do not hold any data

---

## Medical Disclaimer

Protein Tracker is **not a medical application**.

- The App is provided for **informational and educational purposes only**
- It does **not** provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
- It does **not** replace professional medical advice

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making dietary or health-related decisions.

---

## Children’s Privacy

Protein Tracker does not knowingly collect any data from children or adults.

As no data collection occurs, there are no child-specific data concerns.

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be made available within the App or on its store listing.
